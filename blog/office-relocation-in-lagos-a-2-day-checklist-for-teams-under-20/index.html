Small teams cannot afford chaotic office moves. One broken monitor or one lost router can freeze productivity for days.

Who this guide is for

This guide is for business owners and operations teams planning reliable bulk moves across Lagos neighborhoods and traffic corridors.

This 2-day checklist is designed for Lagos teams under 20 people.

Day 1: prep and labeling

Assign one move lead and one backup. Then label everything in three groups:

  • Critical day-one: routers, laptops, extension boards, admin files
  • Operational: desks, chairs, printers, shelves
  • Non-urgent: decor, archived files, extras

Use clear tags per department: admin, product, sales, operations.

Day 1 evening: tech and data safeguards

Before any physical move:

  • backup shared files
  • photograph current cable setups
  • pack routers and adapters in one marked box
  • pack one emergency office kit (chargers, tape, markers)

Most office move delays are tech setup delays.

Day 2 morning: controlled loading

Don't mix critical and non-critical items.

Loading order:

  1. Non-urgent furniture
  2. Operational equipment
  3. Critical day-one kit (last in, first out)

This helps you start work quickly even before full setup is done.

Arrival sequence at new office

First 90 minutes should focus on business continuity:

  • internet and power
  • core workstations
  • finance and admin desk
  • team communication channel reset

After that, proceed with furniture arrangement.

Common office move errors

  • no ownership of move coordination
  • unlabeled shared equipment
  • no cable documentation
  • no priority unpacking order

These are process failures, not logistics failures.

Minimal downtime checklist

  • Team informed of timeline
  • Key assets packed and tagged
  • Internet setup owner assigned
  • One emergency work zone ready
  • Post-move validation done by team lead

Move checklist

  • Group inventory or equipment by priority and fragility
  • Confirm the right vehicle type for total load volume
  • Share destination access notes and receiver contact
  • Keep a move log for timing, cost, and completion quality

Frequently asked questions

Which Sphere vehicle option is best for this kind of move in Lagos?

Use compact car for light, low-volume transfers, van for bulky mixed household or business items, and medium truck for full-load movement or high-volume stock.

How early should I request a driver for a bulk move in Lagos?

For better driver matching and smoother execution, request a driver at least 24 to 48 hours ahead, especially when your move has tight access windows or multiple heavy items.

Can Sphere support recurring business movements?

Yes. Businesses can run repeat movement patterns with standardized load notes, route timing preferences, and clear receiving contacts to improve consistency over time.
