When offices move from Ikeja to Victoria Island, downtime is the hidden cost. The move itself may be one day, but productivity losses can stretch for a week.

Who this guide is for

This guide is for business owners and operations teams planning reliable bulk moves across Lagos, Abuja, and Ibadan.

This playbook helps small and mid-sized teams transfer furniture and equipment with minimal interruption.

Phase 1: pre-move audit

Request a driver Talk to operations

List assets in three groups:

  • work-critical (desks in use, monitors, network tools)
  • important but non-critical (cabinet units, extra chairs)
  • storage/archived items

Tag each item before move day.

Phase 2: loading strategy

  • load archived/non-critical items first
  • keep critical tools last-in for first-out unloading
  • separate fragile office electronics
  • assign one person to verify counts

A clean load order reduces setup time at destination.

Phase 3: destination setup order

  1. Internet and power lines
  2. Core workstations
  3. Team communication zone
  4. Remaining furniture arrangement

Do not start with decoration and layout aesthetics.

Risk-control notes

  • Photograph cable and equipment states before departure
  • Keep client-facing devices in protected cartons
  • Confirm building service access timing
  • Keep facility contact available during unloading

One-day vs two-day method

If your team is under 15 people, one structured day can work. For larger setups with many dependencies, split into two days and protect operations.

Lagos business action block

For Ikeja-to-VI office moves, run a command-style move brief:

  • one move lead
  • one tech lead
  • one receiving lead
  • one fallback communication channel

Prepare a "first 2 hours" list that covers internet, workstations, and admin desk setup. This prevents half-day productivity loss and keeps client-facing teams active quickly.

Where possible, move archive and non-critical furniture first, then move active workstations in a controlled final batch.

Move checklist

  • Group inventory or equipment by priority and fragility
  • Confirm the right vehicle type for total load volume
  • Share destination access notes and receiver contact
  • Keep a move log for timing, cost, and completion quality

Frequently asked questions

Which Sphere vehicle option is best for this kind of move in Nigeria?

Use compact car for light, low-volume transfers, van for bulky mixed household or business items, and medium truck for full-load movement or high-volume stock.

How early should I request a driver for a bulk move in Nigeria?

For better driver matching and smoother execution, request a driver at least 24 to 48 hours ahead, especially when your move has tight access windows or multiple heavy items.

Can Sphere support recurring business movements?

Yes. Businesses can run repeat movement patterns with standardized load notes, route timing preferences, and clear receiving contacts to improve consistency over time.
