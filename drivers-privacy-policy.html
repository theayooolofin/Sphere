Drivers' Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Sphere collects, uses, and protects driver data on our platform.

Last updated: February 13, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect identity, contact, vehicle, and account verification information required to onboard and manage driver activity. This may include your name, phone number, email address, and vehicle documentation.

2. Location Data

Sphere uses location data during active sessions to match requests, estimate arrival times, and help with trip support. Location processing is limited to operational and safety needs.

3. How We Use Driver Data

  • To verify account eligibility and prevent fraud.
  • To assign jobs and optimize dispatch quality.
  • To process earnings, payouts, and support requests.
  • To improve platform reliability and service performance.

4. Data Sharing

We share only necessary move details with customers and authorized service providers. We do not sell personal information to third parties.

5. Security and Retention

Sphere applies access controls and operational safeguards to protect stored data. Driver records are retained as required for operational, legal, and tax purposes.

6. Your Rights

You may request account data review or correction by contacting support through our contact page.
