Delete My Account

You can request deletion of your Sphere account and all associated personal data at any time.

Last updated: March 16, 2026

How to Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your Sphere account in one of two ways:

  1. In-app: Open the Sphere app, go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account and follow the on-screen steps.
  2. By email: Send a deletion request to support@sphere.ng from the email address linked to your account. Include your full name and phone number so we can locate your account.

We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation email once your account and data have been deleted.

What Gets Deleted

When your account deletion request is completed, the following data is permanently removed from our systems:

  • Your profile information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Saved addresses and location history
  • Payment method details
  • In-app messages and support chat history
  • Device tokens and notification preferences
  • Your account credentials and login data

What We Retain

Some data may be retained for a limited period after account deletion where we are legally required to do so:

  • Transaction records — booking history and payment receipts are kept for up to 7 years for tax and financial compliance obligations under Nigerian law.
  • Fraud and safety records — if your account was flagged for suspicious activity, relevant records may be retained for up to 2 years to protect other users.

Retained records are de-identified where possible and are not used for any marketing or product purposes.

Effect of Deletion

  • You will be logged out of the Sphere app immediately.
  • All active or pending bookings will be cancelled.
  • Your account cannot be recovered after deletion is confirmed.
  • If you wish to use Sphere again in the future, you will need to create a new account.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this process or your data rights, contact us at support@sphere.ng. You can also review our Users' Privacy Policy for full details on how we handle your data.
