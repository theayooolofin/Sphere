Users' Privacy Policy

This policy outlines how Sphere handles customer and app user data across bookings and account activity.

Last updated: February 13, 2026

1. Data We Collect

Sphere collects profile data such as name, contact details, pickup and drop-off information, and in-app activity required to complete logistics requests.

2. Booking Information

Request data, including addresses, selected vehicle type, and pricing details, is used to match you with drivers and coordinate safe completion of moves.

3. Why We Process Your Data

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • To notify you about booking status updates and support actions.
  • To prevent misuse, fraud, and unauthorized account access.
  • To satisfy legal and compliance obligations.

4. Payment and Security

Payment processing is handled through secure channels. Sphere does not intentionally expose confidential financial data and applies controls to protect user records.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share limited operational information with verified drivers and core service providers to complete moves. We do not sell user personal data.

6. Contact and Requests

For privacy questions, corrections, or account concerns, contact us via our support page.
